Show your visitor is you are open for business.

== Description ==

Show your visitor is you are open for business with a shortcode.

Example:

    We are [open_for_business].
    
Result: _'we are open'_ or _'we are closed'_.
 
    We are [open_for_business elaborate="1"].
    
Result: _'we are open (open today until 5:00PM).'_ or _'we are closed (open Monday from 9:00 AM).'_.

== Installation ==

Look for the 'Open for business' plugin in the Wordpress Plugin directory.
